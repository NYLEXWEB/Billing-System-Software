1. Introduction

Welcome to EasyToBill ("EasyToBill", "App", "Software", "we", "our", or "us"), a billing and business management application developed and operated by NYLEX, an independent software partnership based in Kozhikode, Kerala, India.

This Privacy Policy explains how EasyToBill collects, uses, processes, stores, and protects information when you use our application, website, and related services.

EasyToBill is designed with privacy as a core principle. Business data created within the application primarily remains under the control of the user. Billing records, inventory, reports, and other business information are intended to be stored within the user's own Google Drive backup system rather than on NYLEX-owned servers, except where necessary to provide authentication, analytics, crash reporting, security, and essential application functionality.

By downloading, installing, registering, or using EasyToBill, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to this Privacy Policy.

If you do not agree with this Privacy Policy, you must discontinue using the application.

6. Google Drive Backup

EasyToBill provides secure backup functionality through the user's own Google Drive account.

Automatic Backup: The application may automatically create scheduled backups of eligible business data to the authenticated user's Google Drive account.
Manual Backup: Users may manually create backups at any time using the "Backup Now" feature.
Restore: Users may restore previously backed-up information after authentication and security verification using their application security password.
User Responsibility: Users remain solely responsible for:
- Maintaining access to their Google Account.
- Maintaining access to Google Drive.
- Protecting their Google Account credentials.
- Protecting their EasyToBill security password.
- Ensuring sufficient Google Drive storage space.

If a user deletes backup files, removes Google Drive access, deletes their Google Account, or otherwise causes backup data to become unavailable, NYLEX cannot recover such data and shall not be responsible for any resulting loss.
