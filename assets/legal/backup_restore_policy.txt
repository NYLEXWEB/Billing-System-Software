EASYTOBILL DATA BACKUP & RESTORE POLICY

Effective Date: July 18, 2026
Last Updated: July 18, 2026
Version: 1.0

---

1. Introduction

This Data Backup & Restore Policy explains how EasyToBill manages the backup and restoration of user business data. It outlines user responsibilities, backup methods, restore procedures, data security, and limitations.

By using EasyToBill, you agree to this Policy.

---

2. Purpose

EasyToBill provides backup and restore functionality to help users protect their business information from accidental loss, device replacement, or application reinstallation.
Backup is intended as a convenience feature and does not replace the user's responsibility to maintain important business records.

---

3. Data Included in Backups

Depending on the version of the application, backups may include:
- Business profile
- Product catalog
- Categories
- Inventory records
- Sales invoices
- Transaction history
- Expense records
- Reports
- Business settings
- Printer settings
- Other application data created by the user

Temporary files, application cache, and system files are generally not included.

---

4. Google Drive Storage

EasyToBill stores backup files in the user's own Google Drive account.
The backup files remain under the user's Google account and are not stored as business data on NYLEX servers.
Users are responsible for maintaining access to their Google account.

---

5. Automatic Backup

EasyToBill may automatically create backups at scheduled intervals when enabled.
Automatic backups may require:
- Internet connectivity
- Google Sign-In
- Available Google Drive storage space
- Required application permissions

Automatic backups may not occur if these requirements are not met.

---

6. Manual Backup

Users may manually create a backup at any time using the backup feature within EasyToBill.
NYLEX recommends creating a manual backup before:
- Updating the application
- Resetting the device
- Reinstalling the application
- Changing devices
- Performing major business changes

---

7. Restore Process

Users may restore previously created backups after verifying their identity, which may include the application security password and Google account authentication.
The restore process may overwrite existing application data. Users should ensure they have a current backup before restoring older data.

---

8. User Responsibilities

Users are responsible for:
- Creating regular backups.
- Protecting their Google account credentials.
- Maintaining sufficient Google Drive storage.
- Verifying backup completion.
- Keeping their security password confidential.
- Confirming restored data is accurate.

Failure to maintain backups may result in permanent data loss.

---

9. Data Security

EasyToBill uses reasonable security measures to protect backup operations.
However, NYLEX cannot guarantee absolute security of data stored in third-party services such as Google Drive.
Users should also protect their devices with passwords, screen locks, or biometric authentication.

---

10. Data Loss

Although EasyToBill is designed to help protect business data, NYLEX cannot guarantee that backups will always be successful or that data loss will never occur.
NYLEX is not responsible for data loss resulting from:
- User error.
- Deleted backup files.
- Google account issues.
- Internet failures.
- Device damage or theft.
- Corrupted storage.
- Third-party service interruptions.
- Failure to perform backups.

---

11. Changes to this Policy

NYLEX may update this Data Backup & Restore Policy from time to time. The latest version will be published through the EasyToBill application, official website, or other official communication channels.
