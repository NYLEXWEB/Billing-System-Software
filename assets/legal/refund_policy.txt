1. Introduction

This Refund & Cancellation Policy explains the terms and conditions under which refunds may be requested and processed for the EasyToBill software license, developed and operated by NYLEX.

---

2. Initial Refund Period (Within 24 Hours)

Customers may request a refund within 24 hours of software activation, provided that:
- The request is genuine.
- No fraudulent activity is involved.
- The software has not been misused.
- The customer cooperates with any verification requested by NYLEX.

If approved, the full software amount may be refunded.

---

3. Refund Requests After 24 Hours (Within 7 Days)

Refund requests made after the initial 24-hour period and within 7 calendar days will be reviewed individually.
If approved:
- Installation, setup, configuration, onboarding, travel, or service charges may be deducted before processing the refund.
- The deduction amount will depend on the services already provided.

---

4. No Refund After 7 Days

Except where required by applicable law:
- Software purchases become non-refundable after 7 calendar days from activation.
- Continued use of the Software after this period constitutes acceptance of the product.

---

5. Refund Exclusions

Refunds will generally not be granted for:
- Change of mind or lack of product knowledge.
- User negligence.
- Device incompatibility not disclosed before purchase.
- Internet connectivity issues.
- Printer issues unrelated to EasyToBill.
- User-entered data errors.
- Failure to create backups.
