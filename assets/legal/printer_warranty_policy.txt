1. Introduction

This Printer Warranty Policy explains the warranty terms applicable to receipt printers sold or supplied by NYLEX together with the EasyToBill software.
This Policy applies only to hardware purchased directly from NYLEX or an authorized reseller.

By purchasing a printer from NYLEX, you agree to this Printer Warranty Policy.

2. Warranty Coverage

Unless otherwise stated in writing, eligible receipt printers include a limited manufacturer's warranty of up to one (1) year from the date of purchase, subject to the manufacturer's warranty terms and applicable law.
The warranty covers manufacturing defects and hardware failures that occur during normal use.
The exact warranty period may vary depending on the printer manufacturer and model.

---

3. What Is Covered

Subject to inspection and approval, the warranty may cover:
- Manufacturing defects.
- Hardware component failures.
- Factory assembly defects.
- Power-related defects not caused by misuse.
- Print mechanism defects caused by manufacturing faults.
- Mainboard failures resulting from manufacturing defects.

Repair or replacement will be determined according to the manufacturer's warranty policy.

---

4. What Is Not Covered

The warranty does not cover damage resulting from:
- Physical damage, accidental damage, or liquid exposure.
- Fire, flood, or electrical surges.
- Improper installation or unauthorized repair or modification.
- Normal wear and tear, cosmetic damage, or use of incompatible accessories.
- Misuse, negligence, or damage caused during transportation after delivery.
- Damage caused by insects, rodents, or environmental conditions.

Consumable items such as paper rolls, batteries (if applicable), cables, adapters, or accessories may not be covered unless specifically stated by the manufacturer.

---

5. Customer Responsibilities

Customers are responsible for:
- Using the printer according to the manufacturer's instructions.
- Keeping the printer clean and properly maintained.
- Using compatible receipt paper and accessories.
- Protecting the printer from moisture, dust, and excessive heat.
- Retaining proof of purchase for warranty claims.

Failure to follow recommended operating procedures may void the warranty.

---

6. Warranty Claim Process

To request warranty service, customers should contact NYLEX using the official support channels and provide:
- Purchase invoice or receipt.
- Printer model and serial number (if available).
- Description of the issue.
- Photos or videos of the problem, if requested.
- Any additional information reasonably required for diagnosis.

NYLEX may perform initial troubleshooting before approving warranty service.

---

7. Inspection and Service

Warranty claims are subject to inspection by NYLEX, the manufacturer, or an authorized service center.
If the issue is determined to be covered under warranty, the printer may be repaired or replaced at the manufacturer's discretion.
Replacement units may be new or manufacturer-refurbished, where permitted by law.

10. Out-of-Warranty Repairs

If a printer is found to be outside the warranty period or the issue is not covered by warranty:
- Repair services may still be available.
- Charges for parts, labor, and shipping may apply.

11. Replacement Policy

A replacement printer may be provided only if:

- The manufacturer authorizes replacement.

The replacement of a printer does not automatically extend the original warranty period unless required by the manufacturer or applicable law.
