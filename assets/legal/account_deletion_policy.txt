EASYTOBILL ACCOUNT DELETION POLICY

Effective Date: July 18, 2026
Last Updated: July 18, 2026
Version: 1.0

---

1. Introduction

This Account Deletion Policy explains how users can permanently delete their EasyToBill account, what data will be deleted, what data may be retained, and the consequences of account deletion.

By using EasyToBill, you acknowledge and agree to this Policy.

---

2. Requesting Account Deletion

Users may request permanent deletion of their EasyToBill account through the application's Account Deletion feature.
For security purposes, users may be required to:
- Sign in with their registered Google Account.
- Enter their EasyToBill security password.
- Complete any additional identity verification reasonably required by NYLEX.

---

3. What Happens After Deletion

Once an account deletion request is confirmed:
- The EasyToBill account will be permanently deleted.
- Access to the application using that account will be removed.
- User profile information stored by EasyToBill will be deleted, subject to Section 6 of this Policy.
- Deleted accounts cannot be restored.

---

4. Google Drive Backups

EasyToBill stores backup files in the user's own Google Drive account.
Deleting an EasyToBill account does not automatically delete backup files stored in Google Drive.
Users are responsible for deleting any backup files they no longer wish to keep.

---

5. Data That Will Be Deleted

Subject to applicable law, account deletion may permanently remove:
- Business profile information.
- Application settings.
- Product records.
- Categories.
- Inventory data.
- Sales invoices.
- Transaction history.
- Expense records.
- Reports.
- Printer settings.
- Other user-generated application data associated with the account.

Once deleted, this information generally cannot be recovered.

---

6. Data That May Be Retained

NYLEX may retain certain limited information where required or permitted by applicable law, including for:
- Legal compliance.
- Fraud prevention.
- Security investigations.
- Dispute resolution.
- Enforcement of legal rights.
- Compliance with court orders or government requests.

Such retained information will be limited to what is reasonably necessary.

---

7. Consequences of Deletion

By deleting an account, the user acknowledges that:
- Access to EasyToBill will end.
- Deleted application data cannot normally be recovered.
- Licenses previously granted may no longer be usable after deletion, unless otherwise required by law or agreed by NYLEX.
- Certain legal obligations may continue after account deletion.

---

8. User Responsibilities

Before deleting an account, users should:
- Create a final backup of important business data.
- Verify that backups are complete.
- Export any records needed for accounting or legal purposes.
- Ensure all required business information has been safely retained.

NYLEX is not responsible for data loss resulting from account deletion requested by the user.

---

9. Fraudulent or Unauthorized Requests

NYLEX reserves the right to refuse, delay, or verify account deletion requests where there is reasonable evidence of:
- Identity fraud.
- Unauthorized account access.
- Ongoing security investigations.
- Legal obligations requiring temporary retention.
- Court orders or regulatory requirements.

---

10. Changes to this Policy

NYLEX may update this Account Deletion Policy from time to time. The latest version will be made available through the EasyToBill application, the official website, or other official communication channels.
